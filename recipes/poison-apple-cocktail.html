Poison Apple Cocktail

Description

Mirror, mirror on the wall, what's the spookiest drink of them all? This delicious, and totally not poison cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 1 Shot Apple Cider
  • 1/2 Shot Bourbon
  • 1/3 Cup Ginger Beer
  • Ice
  • Pomegranate Juice in a syringe
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar mixed with 1 Tsp Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add cider, bourbon, ginger beer, and ice into a cocktail shaker and shake for a few seconds until cold.
  2. Dab the rim of your glass of choice with some ginger beer, then roll in cinnamon sugar.
  3. Pour drink into glass and serve with syringe of pomegranate juice on the side.

Cheers!

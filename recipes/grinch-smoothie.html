The Grinch Smoothie

by Diana Ault at Fiction-Food Cafe

Description

A fuzzy green grinchy smoothie that'll make your heart grow three sizes bigger.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Fresh Dill, stems removed + extra whole sprigs for garnish
  • 3/4 Cup Frozen Chopped Mango
  • 1 1/2 Cup Pineapple Juice
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Clementine, peeled and seeds removed
  • 1/2 Banana
  • 2 Tbsp Agave Syrup
  • 1 Tbsp Lime Juice
  • 1/2 mounded tsp Green Spirulina Powder
  • Red and White Striped Straws

Steps

  1. Put everything except the spirulina into a standing blender. Ice Crush mode first, then blend until smooth.
  2. Pour mixture into a pitcher, then put 2/3 cup back into the blender with the spirulina. Blend until fully incorporated.
  3. Spoon at least 1" of the light green mixture into a cup, then spoon a thinner layer of the dark green mixture on top against the cup sides. No need to spoon the dark green onto the center, just around the sides.
  4. Repeat alternating thicker light green layers and thinner dark green layers, ending with the light green on top.
  5. Slide a skewer down the inside of the cup against the sides and draw it up again repeatedly to create a furry look.
  6. Top with an upright fresh sprig of dill and slide in a red and white striped straw.

Bottoms up!
